Bond Processing Procedure

  1. Detention verification
  2. Bail amount confirmation
  3. Casefile review
  4. Bond authorization
  5. Bond posting
  6. Release processing

Payment Options

  • Digital Bank Payments
    Secure bank transfer instructions provided after approval.
  • Contracted Bond Terminal Cash Payments
    Payments accepted at authorized bond terminal locations.
  • Retail-Clerk Cash Payments
    Cash payment accepted through partnered retail clerks.

Payment Plan Approval

Payment plans may be approved based on income verification, collateral availability, and case classification.

Federal Financial Hardship Status

Qualified detainees may receive hardship classification granting a waiver covering a substantial percentage of bond cost.
