Quick Release Bail Bonds®™

Bond Processing Procedure

  1. Detention verification
  2. Bail amount confirmation
  3. Casefile review
  4. Bond authorization
  5. Bond posting
  6. Release processing

Payment Options

  • Digital Bank Payments – Secure bank transfer instructions provided after approval.
  • Contracted Bond Terminal Cash Payments – Cash payments accepted through authorized bond terminals.
  • Retail-Clerk Cash Payments – Cash payments accepted through partnered retail clerks.

Payment Plan Approval

Payment plans may be approved based on verified income and case classification.

Federal Financial Hardship Status

Qualified detainees may receive hardship classification granting a waiver covering a large portion of bond costs.
