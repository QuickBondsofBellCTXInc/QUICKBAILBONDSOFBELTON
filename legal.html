Legal Notices & Compliance Information

Quick Release Bail Bonds®™ operates under all applicable Texas bail bond statutes, Bell County regulatory standards, and federal financial regulations.

All bond services are subject to underwriting approval, identity verification, and financial review prior to filing with the Sheriff’s Office. Payment methods must clear before bond execution.

2020 Fraud Prevention Act: Clients and cosigners are notified that falsification of information, identity misrepresentation, or attempted unauthorized transactions in connection with bond posting is punishable under federal and state law. Our agency maintains full reporting and auditing capabilities in accordance with this Act.

Rehabilitation and mental health referrals provided are for informational purposes only and do not replace licensed medical, psychiatric, or legal counsel.

Use of mobile or retail payment platforms is subject to verification, availability, and adherence to all applicable financial compliance regulations.
