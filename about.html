About Our Agency

Quick Release Bail Bonds®™ provides professional detention release assistance throughout Bell County and surrounding jurisdictions.

Service charges generally range between 10% – 20% depending on case classification, detention jurisdiction, and risk assessment.

Our mission is to provide efficient and professional service while ensuring compliance with all state and federal financial regulations.
