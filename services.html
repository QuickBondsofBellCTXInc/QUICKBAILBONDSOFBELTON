Payment & Funding Methods

Mobile BankPay®™

Payment Process Information:

  1. You may use your government I.D.–verified mobile banking account to post the bond payment for your loved one.
  2. This payment will be transmitted through your preferred mobile-banking platform into a company account assigned to one of our Financial Administrators.
  3. All necessary instructions and company account credentials for proper payment must be provided directly by your assigned Licensed Bond Agent.
  4. All transactional receipts and payment identifiers will be delivered through secure digital communication methods upon client request.
  5. All bond payments must be confirmed by our Agency’s Financial Department before finalization and placement into the County account with the Sheriff’s Office.
  6. Upon digital and verbal confirmation between company and client, your bond payment becomes active and complete. At that time, follow your assigned Bond Agent’s instructions for the next phase of the release process.

Digital CashPay®™

  1. You may post a bond using physical cash currency at a County-contracted public bonding terminal.
  2. This payment process requires in-terminal I.D. verification as well as secondary identity confirmation through your mobile device.
  3. All procedural steps must be completed strictly under the direction of the Licensed Bond Agent assigned to the active casefile.

Retail Clerk CashPay®™

  1. Your assigned Bond Agent will locate and provide you with the nearest participating retail location (such as CVS®™, Walgreens®™, or another approved merchant).
  2. You will be issued a secure company payment barcode linked to your active casefile.
  3. Present the barcode to the retail clerk and request to load the specified cash amount to the company account.
  4. Provide valid government identification if requested by the retailer for transaction compliance.
  5. Obtain and retain your printed transaction receipt immediately after payment.
  6. Transmit a clear image of the receipt to your assigned Bond Agent for financial confirmation.
  7. Your bond payment becomes active only after confirmation by our Financial Department and direct communication from your assigned Agent.
