Our Payment & Funding Services

Mobile BankPay®™

  1. Use your government I.D.–verified mobile banking account to post the bond payment.
  2. Payment transmitted through your preferred mobile-banking platform to our Financial Administrators.
  3. All instructions and company account credentials provided directly by your assigned Licensed Bond Agent.
  4. Transactional receipts and payment identifiers delivered via secure communication upon client request.
  5. All bond payments must be confirmed by our Financial Department before placement into the County account with the Sheriff’s Office.
  6. After digital and verbal confirmation, follow your assigned Bond Agent’s instructions for the next phase of release.

Digital CashPay®™

  1. Post a bond using physical cash at a County-contracted public bonding terminal.
  2. Requires in-terminal I.D. verification and secondary identity confirmation via mobile device.
  3. Follow instructions strictly from your assigned Licensed Bond Agent for the active casefile.

Retail Clerk CashPay®™

  1. Your Bond Agent provides the nearest participating retail location (CVS®, Walgreens®, or approved merchant).
  2. Receive a secure company payment barcode linked to your casefile.
  3. Present barcode to the retail clerk and load the cash amount to the company account.
  4. Provide government ID if requested for compliance.
  5. Retain your printed transaction receipt and transmit an image to your Bond Agent for confirmation.
  6. Bond payment becomes active after confirmation from our Financial Department.

Major Card Processing

  • Mastercard®™

Cardholder verification is required. Processing fees and authorization review may apply. Funds must clear prior to bond execution.
