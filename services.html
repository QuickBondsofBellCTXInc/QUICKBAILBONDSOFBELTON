Payment & Funding Methods

Mobile BankPay®™

  1. Use your government I.D.–verified mobile banking account to post the bond payment.
  2. Payment transmits through your mobile-banking platform into a company account assigned to a Financial Administrator.
  3. All account credentials are provided directly by your Licensed Bond Agent.
  4. Receipts and payment identifiers are delivered securely upon request.
  5. Bond payments must be confirmed by our Financial Department before placement with the Sheriff’s Office.
  6. Once confirmed, follow your Bond Agent’s instructions for release coordination.

Digital CashPay®™

  1. Post a bond using physical cash at a County-contracted public bonding terminal.
  2. I.D. verification and secondary confirmation via mobile device required.
  3. Follow all steps under direction of your Licensed Bond Agent.

Retail Clerk CashPay®™

  1. Your Bond Agent will provide the nearest participating retail location (e.g., CVS®™, Walgreens®™).
  2. Receive a secure company payment barcode linked to your casefile.
  3. Present the barcode to the retail clerk and load the cash amount.
  4. Show valid government ID if requested.
  5. Keep your receipt and send a clear image to your Bond Agent.
  6. Payment is active only after Financial Department confirmation.

Major Card Processing

  • Visa®™
  • Mastercard®™

Cardholder verification required. Processing fees may apply. Funds must clear prior to bond execution.
